A full case-study deck, hosted separately — this entry just points at it.

The exercise: take Make's existing building blocks (Scenario Properties, Teams,
Analytics, Grid) and ask what it would take to turn scattered metadata into a
structured, searchable portfolio-level data model — one flexible enough that
dashboards, governance agents, and future MCP consumers could all draw from the
same underlying layer without expanding the scope of analytics itself.

The deck walks through the full arc: context and market shift, why now,
personas and customer research, a gap analysis against existing capabilities,
problem statement, proposed solution and UX, success metrics, and a phased
roadmap — plus a follow-up section sketching what's possible beyond analytics
alone: an insight engine and executive/operations agents built on the same
foundation.
